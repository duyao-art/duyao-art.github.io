DU Yao is a Ph.D. student at [The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology](https://hkust.edu.hk/), supervised by [Prof. Xiaomeng LI](https://xmengli.github.io/). He is broadly interested in computer vision, deep learning and medical image analysis.
